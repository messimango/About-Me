Volleyball


I love playing volleyball. I prefer playing outside on the grass but the beach is a close second. In the winter when the other options are limited by weather there are also many indoor courts that I play in. There are many great thing about playing volleyball. It is a great workout for your whole body and it also helps improve your reflexe, balance and hand-eye coordination. It also teaches youabout teamwork and communication making it a great social activity. I as also a part of many diffrent teams, some play for fun and are very beginner friendly and others can get super competitive.
